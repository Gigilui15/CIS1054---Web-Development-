Wild West Bar and Grill Staff and Story:

Wild West Bar and Grill was founded back in 1996 when Jake Smith embarked upon a project of opening our Restaurant. Being family-based, the restaurant aims mainly to serve delicious traditional Western food from the heart.

Many Events are hosted at Wild West Bar and Grill such as Kareoke nights, Bingo Knights, and many more!
